Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a Personalized Race Day Plan?

A Personalized Race Day Plan is a custom-made strategy that helps trail runners prepare for their races by considering their current fitness levels and specific race goals. It includes training plans, race-day tactics, nutrition advice, mandatory gear recommendations, and weather conditions.

Can the Elevated Race Strategy adapt if my goals or the race details change?

Yes, the Elevated Race Strategy can adapt to changes in your goals or race details. The plan can be adjusted based on new information or evolving needs to ensure it remains effective.

How personalized are the training plans and race strategies?

The training plans and race strategies are highly personalized, taking into account your current fitness level, race goals, and individual needs to create a tailored approach.

How will you know my current fitness?

We assess your current fitness through detailed information you provide, including past performance, training data, and any specific metrics relevant to your race goals.
